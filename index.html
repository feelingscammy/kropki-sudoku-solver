Kropki Sudoku Solver

...by feelingscammy

How to Use:

  1. Enter known numbers (1-9) in the cells
  2. Select a dot type:
    • Black Dot: One number is double the other
    • White Dot: Numbers differ by one
    • No Dot: Remove existing dot
  3. Click between cells to place dots
  4. Click "Solve" to complete the puzzle
